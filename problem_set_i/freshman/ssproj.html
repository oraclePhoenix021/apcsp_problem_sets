Big Red Communism Song Parody

This project stands out vividly in my mind from the many projects that we did in this class. It is a parody off of Jason Aldean's Big Green Tractor. I remember struggling to come up with lyrics that made sense, come up with the keys to play on the piano, balance the volumes, and edit the multitudes of short clips together. Despite the struggle, it was a fun project and a deviation from the regular essays or presentations. I did enjoy the silliness of my group's singing, and editing, while difficult, was still fun, especially when the end project was finally finished.
