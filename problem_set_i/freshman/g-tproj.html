Valentine's Day Poem

This was one of the projects from this class that I am especially proud of. Math classes don't generally have any projects, but my teacher did assign a few. This was a math themed Valentine's Day poem. I like the fact that I was able to make it math themed and also make it rhyme, since poetry is not a strong-point of mine. I like the way that my project turned out, and there is nothing that I would do differently.
