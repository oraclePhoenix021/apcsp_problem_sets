Summer Reading Reflection

This was summer homework for this class. It is a reading reflection on Franz Kafka's The Metamorphasis, a story that we read over the summer, as well as two other sources that we viewed. I thought the story was quite interesting, although I didn't like the other two sources much. They were an article presenting statistics on Internet usage, and a TED talk on the impact of technology on society. All three explored the theme of lonliness, something that I think the 21rst century can definitely relate to. I didn't like writing the reflection, since personally, I don't like to write reflections on reading material. I also think that I could have managed my time with all my summer assignments and this reflection better, since I was doing the majority of this reflection the week before the start of school.
