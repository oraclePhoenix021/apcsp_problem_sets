Spontaneous Presentation

This project went ungraded. It was simply a 3 minute long presentation about any topic whatsoever without slides, notecards, or any other supplement. The idea of the project was to help us see where our shortcomings in terms of presenting lay. I think that I did fairly well presenting, my only shortcoming being eye contact. I chose a topic on which I am an expert: Harry Potter. I liked having the freedom to present about whatever I liked, although I did feel uncomfortable without having a slideshow to divert the focus to.
